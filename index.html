About KaDo

Discover the story behind our brand and our commitment to luxury, quality, and sustainability.

Not Just a Brand, A Philosophy

KaDo was born from a simple yet powerful idea: fashion should be more than just clothing. It should be an expression of identity, quality, and conscious living.

Founded in 2020, we set out to create a luxury fashion brand that breaks the mold. Our white and gold collections represent purity and excellence, while our commitment to sustainable practices ensures we leave a positive impact.

Payment FAQ

How does Koko Pay work?

Koko Pay allows you to split your purchase into 3 equal, interest-free payments. The first payment is made at checkout, with subsequent payments automatically charged every 30 days.

Is WhatsApp payment secure?

Yes, all WhatsApp communications are end-to-end encrypted. For bank transfers, we provide secure payment details and confirm receipt immediately.

Can I change my payment method after ordering?

Yes, contact our customer service within 24 hours of placing your order to change your payment method.
